DomGuardianAI

End‑to‑end data lakehouse with orchestration, analytics, and MLOps.

A production‑ready architecture that ingests financial and news data (news feeds, XBRL 10‑K/8‑K, ticker/NASDAQ, technical analysis), lands to ADLS Gen2 in Parquet, queries via Synapse Serverless with external tables/pyodbc, orchestrates with Durable Azure Functions and queue‑based triggers, and serves analytics in Power BI—pairing classic ML (logistic regression, random forest, LSTM) with Azure OpenAI for conversational and generative use cases.
