About Me

I design and run cloud data platforms that turn fragmented line-of-business data into trustworthy, self-serve analytics. My focus is measurable outcomes for executives and engineering leaders: faster time-to-insight, lower total cost of ownership, and clear dashboards teams rely on.

I am experienced in the Azure ecosystem, including Synapse (serverless and dedicated), ADLS Gen2, Data Factory, and Azure Functions built on Python and SQL to deliver governed datasets and Power BI solutions across Finance, Compliance, Operations, and Executive leadership. I lead initiatives end-to-end: architecture, implementation, observability, and enablement.
