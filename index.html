About Me

I design and operate Azure-first data platforms that turn fragmented line-of-business data into trustworthy, self-serve analytics. My focus is measurable outcomes for executives and engineering leaders: faster time-to-insight, lower total cost of ownership, and dashboards teams rely on.

I work across Synapse (serverless & dedicated), ADLS Gen2, Data Factory, and Azure Functions—with Python and SQL—to deliver governed datasets and Power BI solutions for Finance, Compliance, Operations, and Executive leadership. I lead initiatives end-to-end: architecture, implementation, observability, and enablement.
