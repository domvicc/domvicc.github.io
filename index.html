About Me

Seasoned data-engineering leader who turns fragmented, multi-system business data into a governed, analytics-ready asset on Microsoft Azure. Brings 10+ years of delivering reliable, self-serve insights for Finance, Compliance, Operations, and executive teams—accelerating time-to-value while controlling cost.

Builds and operates Azure data estates end-to-end: Synapse (serverless & dedicated) over ADLS Gen2, event-driven pipelines with Data Factory and Azure Functions (including Durable orchestrations), and reusable Power BI semantic models. Integrates ERP/CRM/financial systems (NetSuite, Salesforce, Intacct, Invoiced) and external sources via APIs/SFTP; implements RBAC/RLS governance, logging and monitoring with Application Insights/Azure Monitor, and robust data-quality checks. Known for clear stakeholder cadence, hands-on delivery, and pragmatic cost engineering (e.g., standing up an enterprise data lake serving 65+ employees while keeping monthly spend lean). Recent work includes automation and gen-AI workflows wired into reporting products, plus leadership of cross-functional migrations and audit programs that uncover material savings. Recognized with company MVP honors and advanced Power BI certification.
