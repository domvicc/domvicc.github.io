About Me

Senior data engineer with a record of building Azure-first data platforms end-to-end—ingestion, modeling, and analytics—while keeping systems observable, performant, and cost-efficient. I work across Synapse (serverless and dedicated), ADLS Gen2, Data Factory, and Azure Functions with Python and SQL to deliver reliable, self-serve datasets and Power BI experiences for Finance, Compliance, Operations, and Leadership.
